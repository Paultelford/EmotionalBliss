Emotional Bliss Privacy Statement

.........

Emotional Bliss respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. Emotional Bliss provides this Privacy Statement to inform you of our privacy policy and practices. We’ve structured our web sites so that, in general, you can visit Emotional Bliss on the web without identifying yourself or revealing any personal information. Emotional Bliss uses personal information to better understand your needs and interest and provide you with better service. Once you choose to provide us with personal information, you can be assured that it will only be used to support your customer relationship with Emotional Bliss. We take seriously the trust you place in us; we will not sell, rent or lease your personally identifiable information to others.

On our website, you can order products, make information requests and engage in e-mail correspondence. The types of personal information you provide to us on these pages may include name, address, phone, email address, invoicing information, transaction, credit card information and contact details.
Emotional Bliss uses your information to help us understand your needs and interests and provide you with better services. Specifically, we use your information to help you complete a transaction or order, to communicate with you, to provide service and support and to update you on services and benefits. Occasionally we may also use your information to contact you for market research regarding Emotional Bliss products or services. We will give you the opportunity to opt-out of market research and customer surveys. Credit card information is used only for payment processing only, and is not used for any other purposes. Data collected online may also be combined with information you provide through other sources such as product registration, call centre or public events such as trade shows or seminars. Anonymous (non-personal) data is aggregated for reporting about the Emotional Bliss Online web site usability, performance, and effectiveness. It is used to improve the customer experience, usability and site content.
Emotional Bliss do not sell, rent, or lease your personally identifiable information to others. We do not share your personal information with any business partners or any unrelated third party companies unless you give your permission in writing. Emotional Bliss contracts with third party service providers and suppliers to provide complete products, services and customer solutions described in “How we use it”. These suppliers and service providers act on behalf of Emotional Bliss and are bound to the Emotional Bliss Privacy Policy. Suppliers and service providers are required to keep confidential the information received on behalf of Emotional Bliss and may not use it for any purpose other than to carry out the services they are performing for Emotional Bliss. These service providers may change or we may contract with additional service providers in order to provide new services. Emotional Bliss do not share personal information with any other third parties without your permission unless required to do so by law.
Emotional Bliss is committed to ensuring the security of your information. To prevent unauthorized access or disclosure, maintain data accuracy and ensure the appropriate use of the information, Emotional Bliss has in place appropriate physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard the information we collect. We use SSL encryption when collecting or transferring sensitive data such as credit card information, please click on the secure padlock logo to view the validation certificate. Credit card numbers are only used for processing payment and are not used for other purposes. Our efforts to protect against credit card fraud help protect your financial data security.
